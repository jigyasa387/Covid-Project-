COVID-19 and You

Thank you for participating in “Psychology of a global pandemic: COVID-19” a research project being conducted by Chitkara University.

This questionnaire has been designed to assess your awareness of COVID-19, stress and anxiety due to it, and it’s affect on your daily routine.

Details collected during the project will be used solely for research purposes. Your information will be kept confidential and no one will have access to it. Participating in the study is free will.
